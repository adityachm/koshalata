Our Story

Born from the
loom's poetry

Koshalata was founded on one belief — that India's rarest weaving traditions deserve to be worn, celebrated, and passed down. We travel to the heart of India's loom clusters to bring you sarees that carry the soul of their origin.

Every saree is a conversation between the weaver's hands and the wearer's grace.
